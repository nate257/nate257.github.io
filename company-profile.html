Company Profile

Year Established: 2025

Operational Area: DSM/Dodoma, Tanzania

TAHESO is a leading consultancy and training firm specializing in labour laws compliance, noncitizen employment/work permits, business registration, legal adherence, and social security.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide expert consultancy and training services, ensuring businesses, institutions, and individuals adhere to labour laws and compliance requirements.

Vision: To empower employers and employees with in-depth knowledge of labor laws to foster a productive work environment.

Our Journey

  • 2025

    TAHESO was established to provide expert consultancy and training services.

  • 2026

    Expanded services to include compliance advisory and social security solutions.

  • 2027

    Recognized as a leading consultancy firm in Tanzania.
