Welcome to TAHESO

We are a leading consultancy and training firm specializing in labour laws compliance, noncitizen employment/work permits, business registration, legal adherence, and social security.

Key Advisory Areas

  • Workplace Health and Safety

    Employers must ensure a safe working environment, including electrical safety inspections, lifting equipment assessments, risk evaluations, and accident investigations.

  • Employment Laws and Labour Relations

    Understanding rights and obligations under the Employment and Labor Relations Act of 2004, including employment contracts, salary payments, and employee rights.

  • Compliance with Foreign Work Permit Regulations

    Employers hiring foreign nationals must follow legal procedures for obtaining work permits and ensure adherence to relevant laws.

  • Business Registration and Legal Compliance

    Companies must be officially registered and comply with legal requirements to avoid legal challenges and ensure legitimate business operations.

  • Training for Employers and Employees

    Professional training on labor laws, employee participation, negotiation and collective bargaining, employee rights, and effective human resource management.

  • Compliance Advisory Services

    Businesses require expert guidance to ensure adherence to employment laws, workplace safety regulations, and business operational requirements.
